GenZ Introduction

Gen Z, short for Generation Z, refers to individuals born roughly between the mid-to-late 1990s and the early 2010s. As digital natives, they have grown up in a world dominated by smartphones, social media, and instant access to information. This generation is known for its diversity. Gen Z has also developed a unique set of slang terms, often influenced by internet culture, memes, and viral trends.
